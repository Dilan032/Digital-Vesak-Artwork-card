  • පියවර 1: Select a theme from the classic 6 spiritual presets (Lanterns, Stupa, Lotus, Samadhi Buddha, Dhamma Chakra or Pahan Pela).
  • පියවර 2: Type the recipient's name (To) and your own name (From). You can also tap one of our quick pre-written Sinhala blessings!
